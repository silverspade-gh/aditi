The places table is a database table in database aditi describing the places R and I have visited or plan to visit over time.

The file used to import entries is called places.csv and is located in this repository.

The date_visited attribute is mandatory if the visited entry is a "Y" (or "y").

Columns (in order of appearance):

name - words that designate and describe the place
address - the location of the place
city - the municipality in which the place is located
state - the province or territory of the political community governed
country - the nation of the political community governed
visited - a boolean describing whether the place has been experienced in person or not
date_visited - the date visited (which is a mandatory entry if 'visited' is 'Y' (or 'y')
notes - commentary on the place
